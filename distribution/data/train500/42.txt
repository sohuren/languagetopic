Katie was excited today because she was going to make her favorite meal!<s>
There were three things Katie had to do to get ready to make her favorite meal.<s>
First, she had to go to the store.<s>
Second, she had to cook her meal.<s>
Third, she had to eat it!<s>
Katie's favorite meal was pizza, but she liked to make it at home all by herself.<s>
First, she went to the store.<s>
Katie bought four things: a pizza crust, tomato sauce, cheese and pepperonis.<s>
She put it all in her car and drove it home!<s>
Next, she turned on her oven and put the sauce on the pizza crust.<s>
She sprinkled the cheese all over the pizza and then put the pepperonis on top.<s>
Katie put the pizza in the oven for half an hour.<s>
When she took it out, it was done!<s>
Katie was very happy.<s>
She ate 3 slices of pizza and went to bed.<s>
