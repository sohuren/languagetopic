Brendan loves cats.<s>
He owns 8 cats.<s>
He has 7 girl cats and only 1 boy cat.<s>
Brendan brushes the cats' hair every day.<s>
He makes sure to feed them every morning and evening and always checks to see if the cats have water.<s>
Sometimes he feeds them special treats because he loves them.<s>
Each cat gets 3 treats.<s>
He doesn't give them food like chips and cake and candy, because those foods aren't good for cats.<s>
He likes to play with the cats.<s>
The cats like to chase balls of paper that Brendan makes for them.<s>
Some of his cats have orange fur, some have black fur, some are spotted and one is white.<s>
The white cat is Brendan's favorite.<s>
She is the first cat he owned.<s>
Her name is Snowball.<s>
When he first got Snowball she was a kitten.<s>
His other cats are named Fluffy, Salem, Jackie, Cola, Snickers, Pumpkin and Whiskers.<s>
