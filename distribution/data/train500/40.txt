David bought a new house and wanted to start a garden in his backyard.<s>
He asked his friend Anthony to go with him to the store.<s>
David and Anthony went to the store on Saturday to pick out soil and seeds.<s>
They went into the big store and passed by many other things like jewelry, books, and movies, and then they reached the garden section.<s>
The store's garden section was huge!<s>
They had vegetable seeds, plant seeds, and flower seeds.<s>
David wanted to grow cucumbers, tomatoes, lettuce, corn, and potatoes.<s>
Anthony helped him find those vegetable seeds.<s>
The next day, David started to plant the seeds.<s>
In a few weeks, there were lots of vegetables growing in his garden!<s>
He began to pick the vegetables and use them when he cooked.<s>
He also gave them away as gifts to his family and friends.<s>
They loved his vegetables!<s>
Soon, David wanted to make his garden even bigger.<s>
He went back to the garden store and bought seeds to plant more vegetables.<s>
Soon his whole backyard was full of delicious vegetables!<s>
