Princess Ellen wanted nothing more than to be a singer when she grew up.<s>
She had a beautiful voice and everyone who heard it said she was the best singer in the land.<s>
But her uncle believed singing would keep her from her job as princess, so he found a witch and paid her to steal Princess Ellen's voice.<s>
The witch made a spell which gave Ellen the witch's voice.<s>
The spell also gave Ellen's voice to the witch.<s>
The witch went on to become famous as a singer, and Ellen grew up to be Queen.<s>
One day Queen Ellen heard of a singer who was the best in the land.<s>
She went to hear this singer, and was surprised to hear her own voice coming from the woman on stage.<s>
When the show was over, Ellen found the singer and gave her a penny.<s>
Ellen told the singer, "You have a magical voice".<s>
The witch was so touched by Ellen's kindness, that she gave Ellen her voice back.<s>
