He normally wakes up around 7 in the morning.<s>
He has a dog named Gob.<s>
Gob is a very smart dog.<s>
Bob also owns a farm.<s>
He grows potatoes on it.<s>
One day he went out with his dog to go check on the farm.<s>
There they found a wild duck, a cat, and a pumpkin.<s>
Gob started to chase the duck.<s>
The duck flew away.<s>
Gob started to get hungry.<s>
Gob then ate the pumpkin.<s>
Bob got mad at Gob for eating the pumpkin.<s>
Bob then threw Gob into the house.<s>
Bob then grabbed a bucket.<s>
He started to collect as many potatoes as he could find.<s>
Bob loved potatoes.<s>
He collected around 600 potatoes!<s>
Bob started to run out of room for all the potatoes he found.<s>
He then started to eat as many as he could.<s>
In 1 hour he ate 100 potatoes!<s>
He got so full he took a nap.<s>
When he awoke he found the cat eating all the potatoes!<s>
The cat ate 200 potatoes before Bob awoke.<s>
However, Bob was too full and fat to move.<s>
The cat laughed and wanted to eat the potatoes again.<s>
Just then, Gob comes running out to chase the cat away!<s>
The cat was too fat to run away.<s>
It had eaten too many potatoes!<s>
Gob grabbed the cat with his teeth and tossed the cat down a well.<s>
Gob became a hero for saving the potatoes!<s>
Bob now lets Gob eat whatever he wants, including pumpkins!<s>
Gob is a very happy dog now!<s>
