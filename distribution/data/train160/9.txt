In a far away land known as Board, there was a great man named Pawn.<s>
This was a very odd land because the only thing to do was play chess.<s>
Pawn did not very much care for chess as he was very bored with it.<s>
Another problem Pawn had was that he didn't like some of the other people in this land.<s>
They had names like Bishop, Queen, Rook, and Knight.<s>
And most of all, there was King.<s>
He was the greatest person in Board.<s>
One day, during their normal battle, Pawn saw an opening to move to the far end of Board.<s>
He almost got hurt by Knight but he got away.<s>
Amazingly, when he got to the other end he became as powerful as Queen.<s>
With this amazing power, Pawn chose to help keep King safe.<s>
All was well until Pawn woke up and knew that it was only a dream.<s>
He was still Pawn.<s>
