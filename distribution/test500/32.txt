The Baseball players name is Tyler.<s>
Tyler was very cool and had a lot of friends.<s>
Tyler could throw a baseball in many different ways.<s>
His favorite way to throw it was fast.<s>
Other people liked to throw it slow, some people liked to throw it on their head, and some people even rolled it on the ground.<s>
Not Tyler, whenever he picked up a baseball he threw it very fast.<s>
People from all over the world came to see Tyler throw the baseball.<s>
He met a guy named Peter, who liked him a lot.<s>
Peter made a lot of people, like his friends Lucy and Jerry, give Tyler things for being good at baseball.<s>
Tyler was so good at baseball people gave him a big house.<s>
He liked a lot of things about the house.<s>
He liked the movie theatre, the bowling place, and the swimming pool.<s>
However his favorite thing about the house was the trampoline.<s>
Tyler got all of these things because he could throw a ball fast, he thought it was very cool.<s>
