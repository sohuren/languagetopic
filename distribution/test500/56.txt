He woke up to his dog barking at him.<s>
His parents gave him a ride to school.<s>
When he arrived at school, he went to his first class which was gym class.<s>
In gym class, his gym teacher made him run laps around the track because he had been late to class.<s>
After he had ran his laps, he played baseball with his friends.<s>
Bob had wanted to play kickball or basketball, but his friends had chosen to play baseball.<s>
After gym class was over, he went to English class.<s>
In English class, he read a story about a lady who had fallen in love with a kitty.<s>
One day the kitty jumped out of the window in the lady's house and never came back.<s>
The lady was sad and wondered why her kitty would leave her.<s>
Bob was very moved by the story.<s>
After English class was over, he went to his third class of the day which was math.<s>
Bob kept quiet in his math class because he didn't know the answers to most of the questions the teacher was asking.<s>
After his math class, he went to the fourth and last class of the day which was arts and crafts.<s>
In arts and crafts, Bob painted a picture that he was very proud of.<s>
When art class was over, Bob was happy because he had finished his first day of school.<s>
He was excited to go to school on Tuesday and Wednesday.<s>
