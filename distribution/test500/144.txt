A man got fired from his job.<s>
He was in such a bad mood after.<s>
He walked out the door with his gum and gloves.<s>
He got madder and madder.<s>
A mailman walking by asked him how his day had been.<s>
He pushed the mailman who fell on a teacher.<s>
A nearby baby started crying.<s>
The man then felt bad and apologized.<s>
He knew the only thing that could make him happy would be pancakes.<s>
He went to a pancake restaurant and ordered a stack of pancakes.<s>
He ate the pancakes he had put syrup on and helped a worker sweep the floor.<s>
He had forgotten all about the firing from his other job.<s>
The worker was so surprised with how helpful the man was he told his boss.<s>
The boss talked to the man and asked if he'd like a job there.<s>
The man was so happy that his day had made such a turn around!<s>
He took the job and became head pancake maker.<s>
