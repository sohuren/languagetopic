They were not friends with Kimmi, a girl in the same class.<s>
And they were not friends with Joe.<s>
Joe was not their friend because he didn't talk to Sam and Jenny.<s>
Joe did not talk to anyone.<s>
Kimmi talked to everyone, but she did not keep secrets she was told and she said  bad things about the people around her all the time.<s>
No one liked her.<s>
Everyone thought she was mean.<s>
One day, Sam and Jenny were playing with their puppy near a big tree when they found Kimmi sitting under the tree crying.<s>
Kimmi was sad that no one wanted to be her friend.<s>
Sam and Jenny felt bad for Kimmi.<s>
Jenny told Kimmi  she would have a lot of friends if she didn't say such mean things about people.<s>
Sam said that was not true, because Joe did not talk about people and he had no friends.<s>
Sam and Jenny thought about it a long time.<s>
Sam and Jenny brought Kimmi to meet Joe.<s>
Kimmi and Joe became friends soon and Kimmi learned not to say mean things about people.<s>
Everyone was happier.<s>
